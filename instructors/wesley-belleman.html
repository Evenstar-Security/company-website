Introducing Marcus Thompson, a remarkable individual born and raised in the vibrant state of Alabama, and currently calling Jacksonville, Florida his home. As an African American man, Marcus has overcome various challenges throughout his life, rising above adversity to become a true inspiration to all.

Marcus's journey in service began with the Active Duty Navy, where he dedicated 12 years of his life as an IT specialist. His unwavering commitment and exceptional skills earned him the rank of Senior Chief in the Navy Reserves, where he continues to contribute his expertise and leadership.

Transitioning into the civilian sector, Marcus found his true calling in the field of cybersecurity. Today, he serves as a highly respected cybersecurity consultant at Ernst and Young, leveraging his extensive knowledge and experience to safeguard organizations against digital threats. With his strategic insights and technical acumen, Marcus has become an invaluable asset to the firm and its clients.

Passionate about making a positive impact, Marcus took a bold step and founded Evenstar Security, a groundbreaking cybersecurity training company. Guided by his vision of inclusivity and empowerment, Evenstar Security offers free and discounted training programs specifically tailored to support military service members and veterans. Through his company, Marcus aims to bridge the knowledge gap, ensuring that individuals who have served their country have access to the skills and resources needed to thrive in the cybersecurity industry.

Marcus's remarkable achievements and dedication to his community have not gone unnoticed. His commitment to excellence, both in his professional endeavors and philanthropic initiatives, serves as a shining example for aspiring cybersecurity professionals and individuals striving to make a difference.

Despite the challenges he has faced along his journey, Marcus continues to inspire others through his resilience, determination, and unwavering belief in the power of education and opportunity. As a proud Alabamian and resident of Jacksonville, he embodies the spirit of success, leadership, and community, leaving an indelible mark wherever he goes.
